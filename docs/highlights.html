Effects of seed storage time and salt stress on the germination of Jatropha curcas L.

Highlights

  • Reduction of water in the interstice of the seeds in J. curcas helps to maintain seed viability and oils content.

  • The NaCl in the irrigation water can affect the seedlings since 75 mM, producing reduction in the biometrics and germination parameters.

  • Exist a different levels of tolerance between the genotypes of J. curcas from different regions of Brazil

  • The biometric parameter explain better J. curcas behavior under salinity condition.
